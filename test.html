Welcome to CalcuGuess, the terrible calculator guessing game

DESCRIPTION:

This "game" will take two whole numbers of the player's choosing and select a random mathematical operator to use.
The player then has to choose which operator the computer chose to come up with the shown result.

RULES:

1. Must enter whole numbers

2. AI will only choose the operator that will give a whole number answer





RESULT:

GUESS THE OPERATOR
